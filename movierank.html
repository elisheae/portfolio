3 Comedies Definitely Worth Watching

Are they my favorites? Maybe. It's hard to choose.

Arsenic and Old Lace

From 1944, this film adaptation of Joseph Kesselring's morbid play never fails to make me laugh.

Pineapple Express

A ridiculous, far-fetched stoner comedy, replete with Buster Keaton-worthy slapstick fights. What's not to like?

History of the World, Part I

In case you didn't know, Mel Brooks is the king of satire. As we learn from the French Revolution portion of this classic, "It's good to be the king."
